It has become a platitude to lament the decline of the Internet of Yore. Platforms backed by Big Tech have come to control the way we create, disseminate, and 'consume' 'content,' but -- as the [small web movement](https://ar.al/2020/08/07/what-is-the-small-web/) argues -- it doesn't have to be that way. Here are some great (personal) websites that attest to that; they prove that there's plenty of interesting stuff to be found online outside of hyperoptimzied recommendation algorithms, so add them to your RSS reader.
